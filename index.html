Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our services and requirements for getting a 3D real estate on your website? You might want to look through this short list of questions and answers. If you still have inquiries, don't hesitate to contact us.

  • What are the initial requirements I need to send to start a project? expand_more expand_less

    To be able to create a Web 3D Home of your real estate properties, we need these three items:

    ● Floor Plan of the house in PDF or CAD format
    ● Elevation view in PDF or CAD format, with materials specified and shown as an image
    ● Other sketches or design inspiration images and files that will help us better capture the design in mind

  • How long is the delivery time? expand_more expand_less

    The standard turnaround time for a Web 3D Home is:

    ● 3-5 working days for a single-story property, and
    ● 5-8 working days for a two-story real estate property

    However, it depends on the quality, complexity, and details of real estate services you want. 3D interactive with customizable colors, finishings, and textures will take longer. For an approximation of the timeline for your specific requirements, please contact us.

  • What is the final output or type of file will I get? expand_more expand_less

    You will receive the files , but if they are too large, we will set-up a download link where you can get these files:

    ● 3D file in GLB or OBJ
    ● 1-2000px .tif/png, hi-res rendering for social media

  • Where and how can we use this 3D file? expand_more expand_less

    Use the file and directly embed it on your website, there’s no need to install an app or program. You can also use our 3D files on social media platforms that support 3D output like Facebook. You will receive detailed embedding instructions along with the 3D files.

  • What is your payment schedule? expand_more expand_less

    We require 25% prepayment upon initial agreement and signing of the contract, and 75% upon completion of the project and final product delivery.
